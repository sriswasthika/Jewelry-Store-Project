Cleaning Your Jewelry

Keep your jewelry sparkling with these simple home remedies:

Gold & Silver: Use a mixture of warm water and mild dish soap. Soak for 15 minutes, then gently scrub with a soft toothbrush.

Diamonds: Mix baking soda with water to form a paste, apply it, and rinse after a few minutes.

Fixing a Broken Chain

If your necklace or bracelet chain breaks, try these methods:

Use small pliers to reconnect open links.

For minor separations, apply a drop of clear nail polish as a temporary fix.

Removing Knots from Chains

Knotted chains can be frustrating! Try this trick:

Apply a small amount of baby oil to loosen the knot, then use a sewing needle to gently untangle.

Storing Your Jewelry

Prevent tarnish and scratches with proper storage techniques:

Keep pieces in a soft pouch or lined jewelry box.

Use anti-tarnish strips for silver jewelry.
